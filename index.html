Selected product engagements.

Each engagement began as a complex, high-stakes problem. The method is consistent: locate the real bottleneck, design the smallest solution that proves the bet, then scale it. Clients are described by sector for confidentiality.

01Global pharmaceutical organisation · AI / ML

ML-assisted clinical document authoring platform (0-to-1)

Problem

4,000+ medical, clinical and regulatory specialists authored compliance-critical documents through email and manual workflows. No fit-for-purpose product existed, and turnaround was slow and error-prone.

Approach

Set the product vision and MVP scope on IBM Watson — defining section priorities, ML task scope, confidence levels and acceptance thresholds. Core principle: automate the repetitive and pattern-heavy, flag uncertainty, keep human judgement on high-risk interpretation.

Outcome

80% faster turnaround (40h → 8h), ~$2M annual savings, launched at 80–90% model accuracy with a human-in-the-loop review architecture.

02UK government agency · Public sector / GDS

Digital case-management service for animal housing (0-to-1)

Problem

Paper records, email approvals and manual reconciliation ran a high-volume regulated operation. Two very different user groups — field staff and admin — were forced through one unfit workflow.

Approach

Led a 12-person multidisciplinary team from discovery to adoption. Ran workshops to map the manual process, designed distinct flows for field and admin users, built on PowerApps to GDS/WCAG standards, and guided OCR for passport data capture.

Outcome

Case handling cut from 10 days to 2, 90% of manual processes automated, errors and backdating down 90%. Recognised with a promotion for leadership and impact.

Portfolio design ethos

Designed to be simple, usable, accessible and sustainable.

These aren't only principles for client work. This portfolio itself was built to follow every one of them.

Accessible by design

This site aims at WCAG 2.2 AA and follows GOV.UK-style patterns: legible contrast in every theme, clear keyboard focus states, a skip link, full keyboard navigation, meaningful alt text and a logical heading order. It respects reduced-motion preferences, and the switch up top offers light, dark and high-contrast modes so you can read it your way.

Light by design

It ships as a single lightweight page — one typeface, one optimised image, no trackers and no heavy frameworks. Less data crosses the network, so it loads fast and carries a smaller energy footprint, and dark mode trims power on modern screens. The same principle applied to product work: build only what earns its place.
